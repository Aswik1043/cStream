1.Take a pan and roast Cumin,poppy seeds,fennel seeds,dry red chillies,cinnamon,cardamom,cloves and coconut


2.Then grind it to fine powder


3.Heat oil in pan and add curry leaves.add the sliced onions and fry till they are light brown then add the crushed garlic and ginger


4.Add the ground spices and star anise and fry for a minute

5.Add the tomatoes, turmeric, salt and stir in the chilli powder.

6.Add the chicken, cover and leave to simmer on the lowest setting until it is tender about 25 minutes.


7.Once chicken is cooked squeeze lime and remove from heat


8.Garnish with coriander leaves
