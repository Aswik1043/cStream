Ingredients

Chicken - 500 grams
Coconut Oil - Â¼ cup
Bay Leaf - 1
Onion - 2 large sliced thinly
Green Chilli - 2 slit
Ginger Garlic Paste - 2 tblsp
Tomatoes - 2 large chopped finely
Turmeric Powder - 1 tsp
Chilli powder - 2 tsp
Garam Masala Powder - 2 tsp
Salt to taste
Coriander Leaves for garnishing

FOR ROASTING & GRINDING:

Oil - 1 tsp Fennel Seeds / Saunf / Sombu - 1 tblsp
Cinnamon / Pattai - 1 stick
Black Pepper - 1 tblsp
Coriander Seeds - 2 tblsp
Onion - 1 large chopped

Instructions

Pressure cook chicken with little salt for 2 to 3 whistle. Set aside.
Heat little oil in a pot. Add in spices and toast them. Take it in a blender.
Add onions and cook till golden brown. Add it with the spices and grind till smooth. Set aside.
Heat oil in a pot, Add in onions, chillies and bay leaf. Cook till golden.
Add in ginger garlic paste and cook till raw smell leaves.
Add in tomatoes and cook till mushy.
Add in ground masala and mix well.
Add salt, turmeric powder, chilli powder and garam masala and cook till oil separates.
Now add in chicken along with any cooking liquid.
Mix well and simmer till oil separates.
Add coriander leaves and mix well.
Serve.
